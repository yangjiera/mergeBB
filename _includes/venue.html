UMAP 2018 will be held at the Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, in the north spine where the School of Computer Science and Engineering is located. NTU's north spine consists of lecture theatres with various capacities, as well as plenty of shops and restaurants.

Singapore is well known as a very beautiful, organized and safe city, with plenty of sightseeing, such as Gardens by the Bay, Marine Life Park: The World's Largest Oceanarium, Universal Studios, Singapore Zoo, Singapore Botanical Gardens, Singapore Flyer, Little India, Sentosa, etc.

Singapore is well connected to the world through its Changi Airport. This airport is one of the busiest and best connected in the world, handling over 100 airlines flying to some 300 cities. The award-winning airport itself is jam-packed with activities and amenities, making it a world-class attraction in its own right.
