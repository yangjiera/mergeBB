Route from Changi Airport to NTU
Singapore Airport: Changi Airport

Route from Airport to NTU by Public Transport

Take the EW line from Airport (CG2) to Tanah Merah Station (EW4),
take the EW line from Tanah Merah to Pioneer Station (EW29).
Then, take Bus 179 from Pioneer station to Lee Wee Nam Station.
Note: You can buy, top up and refund the MRT card (EZ-Link) at any MRT station. All public transports in Singapore will close at 11:30 p.m..


Route from Airport to NTU by Taxi

Around 40 minutes.
Note that the taxi charges will increase after midnight (12:00).
Both Uber and Grab are available in Singapore.
